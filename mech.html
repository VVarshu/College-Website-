MECHANICAL ENINEERING

The Department of Mechanical Engineering is functioning since inception of the college in the year 2011 with an revised intake of 30 each academic year. Department consists of highly dedicated, well qualified and competent faculty members active in teaching, research and building quality Profile of students. Most of the faculty members are from reputed academic institutions with research and industry background contributing to implementation of industry oriented education to students.

ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

The department has laboratories as per University norms for hands-on training and is also into research in the vistas of Mechanical engineering showcasing the ability through quality publications jointly with the students & research scholars. All the labs are developed taking into consideration the needs of academia and industry. Huge investment of over a crore is spent for establishing high profile labs. The department is accredited by the NBA and is thus recognized nationally as one of the youngest department of the institute to be recognized by National Board of Accreditation (NBA for 3 years in 2018). Also, the department has a recognized R&D center by VTU, Belagavi. The Department has funded projects from national funding bodies contributing to significant improvements in the learning environment.
